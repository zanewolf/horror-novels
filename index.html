Spider locations are randomly generated. If problematic occlusion occurs, simply refresh the page to generate new spider coordinates.

The top 20 horror novels were selected from Goodread's Definitive Horror Book List and the data was provided by Zenodo's Best Books Ever Dataset.

Regarding genres: after cleaning to remove genres that were common to every book ('Horror', 'Fiction'), genres that were a combination of two or more genres (e.g. 'Mystery Thriller', 'Science Fiction Fantasy'), 'genres' that described a type of media (e.g. 'Classics','Short Stories', 'Audiobooks', 'Gothic', 'Adult', 'Poetry'), there were 19 remaining. These remaining genres were collapsed into five main categories: Fantasy ('Fantasy'/'Urban Fantasy'); Thriller ('Thriller'/'Mystery'/'Suspense'); Supernatural ('Supernatural'/'Paranormal'/'Vampires'/'Ghosts'/'Zombies'); Scifi ('Science Fiction'/'Dystopia'/'Apocalyptic'/'Post Apocalyptic'); and Drama/Crime ('Drama'/'Crime'). Three of the 19 genres were ultimately not represented, but each was applicable to only one book: 'Religion', 'Historical Fiction', and 'Psychological Thriller'.

Regarding ages: When there were discrepancies between the first publication date provided in the Zenodo dataset and the Goodread's date, the Goodread's date was used.
